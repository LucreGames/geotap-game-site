GEOTAP

Press Kit

Description:

Geotap is Lucre Games’ first mobile launch and was thought to be simple a exercise in shipping a title with the goal creating a platform to launch games with emotionally rich experiences that ride a fine line between pure art experiences and traditional game play.

Features:
  • 3 play modes: Classic, Tranquil and Focus
  • Low pressure game play
  • Smoothing soundscape and colors
  • High score leaderboards
  • 10 simple shapes + 30 unlockable shapes
  • Discover unique interactive shapes
  • Or just relax
